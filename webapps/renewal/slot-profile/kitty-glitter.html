How to play Kitty Glitter

Kitty Glitter is decked out in diamonds and fluffy kitties, but it wouldn't be half as luxurious if it wasn't easy to play! To get started, set your total bet by choosing how many paylines are active and how much you want to bet per line. Spin up a litter of 3 or more cats of the same breed to win; the fancy Persian symbol fetches the highest price. If the wild Kitty Glitter symbol makes an appearance, it can fill in for any cat or minor symbol to complete a win. 3 or more Bowl of Diamonds symbols anywhere on the reels will award 15 spins in the Free Spins Bonus.

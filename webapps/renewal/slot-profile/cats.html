How to play Cats

To start your Cats safari, choose your line bet and how many paylines you want active, and then start exploring. Lions may be the king of the jungle, but the panther is the king of the reels. Lining up 3 or more adjacent big cats across a payline awards a win, with the Panther symbols bringing the biggest rewards when you find 9 or more. With the Split Symbol feature, two Lion, Tiger, Cheetah, Panther, Cougar, and Paw Prints can appear in a single space and count as two symbols. When the wild Cats symbol fills in for a big cat symbol, it will always count as a Split Symbol. Paw Prints are scatter symbols and don’t need to land on a payline to lead you to a prize. 4 Paw Prints will award 2x the total bet, and 5 or more will start the Free Spins Bonus.
